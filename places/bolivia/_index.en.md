# BARK — Bolivian Ark

BARK is a semi-autonomous community planned for Coroico in the Yungas region of Bolivia, at 1,750m elevation. It combines five operational components — [[technopark]], [[trading-house]], [[incubator]], [[retreat-center]], and [[infrastructure]] — into a resilient, self-sustaining settlement that integrates with the local economy rather than replacing it.

## Why Bolivia

Bolivia offers a unique combination of geographic isolation from nuclear targets, low cost of living, favorable cooperative law (Ley 356), rich agricultural output, and cultural traditions of horizontal governance ([[aymara-afro-bolivian|Aymara ayllu system]]). See [[why-an-ark]] for the broader thesis on crisis resilience and [[bolivia-honest-analysis]] for an honest assessment of Bolivia's advantages and limitations.

**Phase 0: Research & Vision.** We are 1-2 people with AI, doing research. No legal entity, no land, no revenue. The website is the deliverable for this phase. See [[roadmap]] for the full timeline.
