# Five Components

BARK stands on five interconnected components. Each supports the others — remove one and the structure weakens.

## 01 — Techno Park

![](/images/icons/technopark.svg)

Open-source R&D laboratory and maker space. Hardware prototyping, software development, appropriate technology research.

See: [[technopark]]

## 02 — Startup Incubator

![](/images/icons/incubator.svg)

Launch ventures that serve both the community and the global market. Mentorship, shared resources, global connectivity.

See: [[incubator]]

## 03 — Trading House

![](/images/icons/trading.svg)

Economic engine connecting local production with regional and global markets. Fair trade, value-added processing, financial resilience.

See: [[trading-house]]

## 04 — Retreat Center

![](/images/icons/retreat.svg)

Technoshamanic retreat blending ancient wisdom with modern practice. Healing, culture, contemplation, creativity.

See: [[retreat-center]]

## 05 — Infrastructure

![](/images/icons/infrastructure.svg)

Semi-autonomous systems for energy, water, food, and connectivity. The physical foundation that makes everything else possible.

See: [[infrastructure]]
