# Build Systems That Matter

![Road carved through the Yungas mountains](/images/yungas/death-road.jpg)

Solar arrays, greywater systems, mesh radios, fabrication labs. Real constraints at 1,750m in the Bolivian cloud forest. Open-source everything.

**Phase 0: Research. No land, no infrastructure, no team on-site yet. This is the planning stage.**

---

## What Gets Built: Six systems. All open-source.

### 01 -- Energy
Solar arrays + micro-hydro + battery storage. Counter-cyclical design for cloud forest climate.

### 02 -- Water
Spring capture, multi-stage filtration, greywater recycling. Gravity-fed, no pumps needed.

### 03 -- Communications
Starlink backbone + LoRa mesh + HF radio. Works when cell towers don't.

### 04 -- Fabrication
3D printing, CNC, Gingery foundry. Print replacement parts, don't order them.

### 05 -- Processing
Coffee roaster, cacao line, solar dehydrators, essential oil stills. Value stays local.

### 06 -- Shelter
Earthship-inspired construction with local materials. Bamboo, compressed earth, recycled tires.

---

## Why Here

> If it works here, it works anywhere.

1,750m altitude. 1,300mm annual rainfall. Landslide season. A single road to the nearest city. These aren't obstacles -- they're the design brief. Every system built here is tested against real failure modes that most maker spaces never encounter.

---

## What do you build?

Solar, water, permaculture, fabrication, mesh networking, firmware. On-site or remote.
