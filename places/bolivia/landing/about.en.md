# What is BARK?

![Yungas valley](/images/yungas/valley.jpg)

**Five systems. One community. Designed for collective ownership.**

The Bolivian Ark. Five linked components generating power, food, shelter, and livelihoods in the cloud forest -- open source, cooperative, rooted in place.

*For anyone encountering BARK for the first time -- potential residents, investors, contributors, or the simply curious.*

---

## Five components, one system

1. **Techno Park** -- A fabrication lab and open-source technology campus where broken pumps get fixed and circuit boards get milled.
2. **Incubator** -- A startup accelerator launching ventures rooted in Coroico's actual soil and actual needs.
3. **Trading House** -- Direct-trade channels for Yungas coffee, cacao, and craft goods -- grower-owned, not middleman-dependent.
4. **Retreat Center** -- Where Aymara healers and biosensor engineers sit in the same room, comparing notes on what the body does during ceremony.
5. **Infrastructure** -- Solar arrays, spring-fed cisterns, mesh radios, vertical growing walls -- autonomy as material fact, not slide deck aspiration.

---

## Where: Coroico, Yungas

1,750 meters in the subtropical crease where the Andes collapse into the Amazon basin. Year-round growing season, 18-28C, abundant spring water. Three hours from La Paz by road -- connected enough to reach, remote enough to function independently.

## Why: Precarity demands capability

The systems most people depend on -- global supply chains, centralized energy grids, just-in-time food logistics -- are optimized for efficiency, not resilience. When they fail, the people with no local capacity to produce are the most exposed.

BARK builds the capability to provision the basics -- power, food, water, shelter, communication, governance -- at a community scale, in a place where the land and climate make it achievable. See [[why-an-ark]] for the full rationale.

## Who

**Started by Drow Dvorkin. Designed to be owned by many.**

Every cooperative begins with someone willing to publish the work before the structure exists. The research is open. The governance model is designed for collective ownership from day one. The team grows as the work proves itself.

## Current Status: Phase 0 -- Research Initiative

No land purchased. No legal entity formed. No revenue generated. What exists: published analysis, open-source plans, a forming community, and a commitment to radical transparency about what is real and what is aspiration.
